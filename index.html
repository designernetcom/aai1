about us

Aai Womens Super Speciality Hospital

Aai Hospital is committed to delivering world-class healthcare by blending advanced medical technology, expert clinical care, and a compassionate approach that places patients at the heart of everything we do. We offer a comprehensive range of super speciality services tailored to the unique needs of women and children. Our specialties include fertility treatments such as Infertility, IUI, and ICSI; advanced maternal care through Obstetrics ICU, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, and Prenatal Counseling; and minimally invasive Laparoscopic Surgeries.. At Aai Hospital, we strive to ensure the best possible outcomes through clinical excellence, innovation, and heartfelt care.

our services

Where Women's Health Meets Compassion and Expertise

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Obstetrics & Gynaecology department provides expert care for women's health, including pregnancy, childbirth, and reproductive services.

Infertility Treatment
Specialized infertility treatments, including advanced diagnostics and assisted reproductive technologies, to help couples conceive.
Laparoscopy
laparoscopy, a minimally invasive surgical procedure that allows for diagnosis and treatment of various conditions with reduced recovery time and minimal scarring.
Painless Delivery
provides painless delivery options, utilizing advanced pain management techniques to ensure a comfortable birthing experience for mothers during labor
Pediatrics
Pediatrics is the branch of medicine that cares for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their growth, health, and development.
NICU
NICU provides advanced care for premature or critically ill newborns, featuring incubators, ventilators, and specialized imaging for comprehensive treatment.
PICU
PICU offers advanced care for critically ill children, with specialized equipment and expert staff for comprehensive treatment
Pediatric Surgery
Surgery provides specialized surgical care for children, ensuring safe and effective treatment for a variety of conditions.
